Erie Day of Code

Venue

Join us at The Brewerie in historic Union Station in downtown Erie!

First built in 1865, Union Station was rebuilt in Art Deco style in 1927 and reopened to the public. 1 The charming rotunda hosted the Union News stand, and many merchants including a soda fountain and barbershop. 2 The Brewerie currently resides in the old rotunda, serving up craft beer, delicious food, and a charming atmosphere.

With its central location, charming atmosphere, and rich history, we can't imagine a better place to host Erie Day of Code!

The Brewerie offers a great meal selection and will be catering the included lunch for the event. If you've never eaten at The Brewerie you're in for a treat!
